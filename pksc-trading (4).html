Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Hard Work

"Started with a vision to serve Indore's local community — grown into the city's most reliable wholesale partner."

PKSC Trading Company was founded in 1995 by Piyush Kumar Subash Chandra with a simple mission — to supply quality local products at fair prices. What began as a modest local supply venture in Siyaganj Market has, over three decades, transformed into a full-fledged wholesale kirana operation trusted by hundreds of businesses.

Today, PKSC proudly serves 300+ businesses across Indore and surrounding areas — from neighbourhood kirana stores and grocery retailers to restaurants, hotels, and institutional buyers.
